Designing Reusable Terraform Modules on AWS

This short article outlines patterns for building reusable Terraform modules for AWS, covering inputs/outputs, locals, workspaces, and a basic CI flow.

Module structure

modules/
  vpc/
    main.tf
    variables.tf
    outputs.tf
  rds/
    main.tf
    variables.tf
    outputs.tf
envs/
  prod/
    main.tf
  dev/
    main.tf

Keep modules focused and composable. Use locals for naming and tags. Validate with terraform validate and tflint. Integrate into CI using a matrix of workspaces.
